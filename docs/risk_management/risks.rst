Risks Analysis
==============

Severity levels
----------------

.. list-table:: Severity levels
   :header-rows: 1

   * - Level
     - Description
   * - Low
     - Negligible impact on safety, health, environment, or operations.
   * - Medium
     - Moderate impact that may require attention but does not pose an immediate threat.
   * - High
     - Significant impact that could lead to severe consequences if not addressed.
   * - Critical
     - Catastrophic impact that could result in loss of life, severe injury, or major environmental damage.
